Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and bustling markets. With its iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate, Delhi attracts tourists from around the world. The city offers a blend of traditional and modern experiences, with historic monuments, vibrant bazaars, and modern shopping malls. Delhi is also a culinary paradise, offering a wide variety of street food and fine dining options. Whether you're exploring ancient architecture or indulging in delicious cuisine, Delhi has something for everyone.
